Nicaragua has all kinds of imaginable beaches, from dark sand to white sand to virgin and wild beaches with an abundance of wildlife refuges. The population is multicultural. According to the literacy campaign, there was a remarkable decrease in the illiteracy rate. Poverty and corruption are major problems.
